1 Business model

NVIDIA designs GPUs and accelerated computing platforms for data centers, AI, gaming and automotive. It sells chips and complete systems, but its real moat is the CUDA software, which locks developers into its architecture. Manufacturing is outsourced (TSMC), keeping its focus on design and ecosystem.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

NVIDIA's competitive advantage forms a near-monopoly in AI accelerators for training. CUDA software lock-in and capacity scarcity grant it exceptional pricing power and low elasticity while supply trails demand.
