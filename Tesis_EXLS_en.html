1 Business model

EXL combines data analytics, artificial intelligence and digital operations services for insurance, banking, healthcare and utilities. It monetizes through recurring services contracts and transformation projects, charging on outcomes, analytics subscription or high-value business process management.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

EXL operates in a highly fragmented data and analytics services market, where it differentiates through vertical specialization and AI capabilities. Although rivalry is intense, its depth of domain in insurance and healthcare lets it sustain relationships and pricing.
