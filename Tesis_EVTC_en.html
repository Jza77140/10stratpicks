1 Business model

Evertec is the leading transaction processor and financial technology provider in Puerto Rico, the Caribbean and Latin America. It generates recurring revenue from merchant acquiring, payment services (including the ATH debit network) and business solutions for banks, charging per processed transaction and software licenses.

Growth drivers: geographic expansion in Latin America via acquisitions; growth of e-commerce and digital payments; and broadening software and processing solutions for financial institutions outside its Puerto Rican base, where cash penetration still leaves room to migrate to electronic methods.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

Evertec's competitive advantage stems from its dominant niche position in Puerto Rico, where it runs the ATH debit network almost as a near-monopoly with long-standing ties to Banco Popular. That critical infrastructure grants it local pricing power, though in Latin America it competes in more fragmented markets.
