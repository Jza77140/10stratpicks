1 Business model

AppLovin monetizes mobile apps through its advertising and AXON intelligence platform, which optimizes the buying and selling of in-app ads in real time. It charges developers to place and optimize campaigns, capturing a share of ad spend. It is shifting from its legacy owned-games business toward higher-margin software.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

AppLovin's competitive advantage operates in a relatively oligopolistic mobile adtech market where it competes with giants. Its AXON engine, fed by proprietary data, generates superior returns that sustain pricing, though its power is less structural and more dependent on keeping its algorithmic edge.
