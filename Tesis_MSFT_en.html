1 Business model

Microsoft monetizes three pillars: Azure cloud and servers; productivity via Office 365 and Dynamics on subscription; and personal computing (Windows, devices, gaming). Most revenue is recurring and sold to enterprises, with growing AI integration (Copilot) across its installed base.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

Microsoft's competitive advantage rests on a cloud oligopoly (alongside Amazon and Google) and a near-monopoly in enterprise productivity. The depth of its ecosystem lowers price elasticity and lets it raise rates and bundle AI without material customer attrition.
Switching costs
Migrating off Office, Windows and Azure is costly and disruptive
