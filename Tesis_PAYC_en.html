1 Business model

Paycom offers a single cloud platform for human capital management: payroll, talent, time and compliance in one system. It monetizes through recurring subscriptions billed per employee per pay period, plus interest on funds in transit, with high margins and retention.

Growth drivers: winning new mid-sized enterprise clients; adoption of its Beti self-service payroll tool, which automates the process; and cross-selling HR modules to raise spend per client.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

Paycom sits within an oligopoly in payroll and HR software, dominated by ADP, Paychex, Workday and Paycom itself. Its single-database architecture and mid-market focus sustain margins and differentiated pricing power.
