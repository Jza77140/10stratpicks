1 Business model

Mastercard operates a payments network connecting issuing banks, merchants and acquirers. It takes no credit risk: it charges per-transaction and volume fees on processed payments, plus value-added services (data, security, consulting). Its revenue grows with consumer spending and the shift from cash to digital.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

Mastercard's competitive advantage rests on an effective duopoly with Visa over global payment rails. The strong network effect among billions of users and merchants creates near-insurmountable barriers and sustains pricing power with low elasticity.

More merchants attract more cardholders and vice versa
