1 Business model

Fair Isaac produces the FICO score, the standard for U.S. consumer credit, licensed to bureaus and lenders on every score inquiry. It complements this with its decisioning software (FICO Platform) for fraud, originations and collections, with recurring subscription revenue and usage-based royalties.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

Fair Isaac's competitive advantage stems from a de facto monopoly: the FICO score is embedded in the regulatory and contractual processes of U.S. credit, granting it extraordinary pricing power and near-zero demand elasticity in its scoring segment.
Market standard
