1 Business model

USLM quarries and mines limestone and processes it into lime and limestone products (quicklime, hydrated lime, slurry and pulverized limestone). It monetizes by selling these to construction, steel, environmental, agriculture and oil-and-gas customers, mostly in regions near its plants.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

USLM's competitive advantage stems from a regional oligopoly: the cost of transporting lime restricts competition to producers near each quarry. This natural geographic protection lowers effective rivalry and grants notable pricing power in its local markets.
