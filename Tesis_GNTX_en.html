1 Business model

Dominant maker of electrochromic auto-dimming mirrors (~90% of the world market) for automakers, with integrated electronics (Full Display Mirror, HomeLink, driver monitoring). Vertically integrated from chemistry to electronics.

3 Competitive advantage (MOAT)

A quasi-monopoly with electrochromic IP and scale: mirrors are designed into each model cycle (multi-year lock-in) and vertical integration makes it very hard to compete on cost and quality. A moat proven over decades.
